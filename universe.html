00 — About

The Story

Where sound becomes vision, and vision becomes world.

Sama Kane is a musician, model, and visual storyteller — a multidisciplinary artist living at the rare intersection of human emotion and machine imagination.

As a singer and songwriter, her music drifts between the cinematic and the intimate — deeply personal, yet sonically expansive. As an AI filmmaker and image artist, she sculpts surreal portraiture and short-form film that interrogate identity, beauty, and the textures of the in-between.

As a creator and curator, she shapes culture from her own atelier — an influencer in the truest sense, defined not by reach, but by taste.

Every release is its own universe. This site is the key inside.
